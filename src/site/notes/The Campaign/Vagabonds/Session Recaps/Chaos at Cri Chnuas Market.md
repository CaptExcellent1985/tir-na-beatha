## Session Summary
The session began with **[[The Campaign/PCs/Exploder\|Exploder]]**, **[[The Campaign/PCs/Koldea\|Koldea]]**, and **[[The Campaign/PCs/Themaer Eagerflower\|Themaer Eagerflower]]** traveling by boat along **[[Geography/Landmark/Cleverclaw Creek\|Cleverclaw Creek]]** toward the [[The Tribes/Kobolds\|kobold]] city of **[[Geography/City/Cri Chnuas\|Cri Chnuas]]** in the **[[Geography/Region/Wellandrym Vale\|Wellandrym Vale]]**. They aimed to secure weapons and traps to combat [[The Tribes/Goblins\|goblin]] raids affecting [[The Tribes/Firbolg\|Firbolg]] settlements. Upon arrival, they encountered a **[[The Tribes/Tritons\|Triton]] Ferryman** who guided them through the cityâ€™s creek tunnel system, where they learned about the market and its trade dynamics.

In the **[[Geography/Locale/Scaled Market\|Scaled Market]]**, the party interacted with various merchants, including **Snarl-Dreg**, a [[The Tribes/Goblins\|goblin]] merchant, and **Nala**, a [[The Tribes/Tabaxi\|tabaxi]] merchant. They attempted to barter for goods but faced challenges, including a refusal from **Nala** to accept textiles in exchange for her wares. The party also discovered that **Ashlika**, a skilled trapper, had been taken by the **Dark Elves** to the **Deadlands** after a dispute. **[[The Campaign/PCs/Koldea\|Koldea]]** took a mislabeled love potion from **Snarl-Dreg** and led the group to the **[[Geography/Locale/Ember-Belly Inn\|Ember-Belly Inn]]** to gather more information.

At the inn, they met **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Spark Scale\|Spark Scale]]**, the proprietor, who provided insights about **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Malachite\|Malachite]]**, an [[The Tribes/Eladrin\|eladrin]] with shifting tattoos. A chaotic incident ensued when a **Ruddy Cheeks McGee** caused a wild magic surge, summoning dust mephits that attacked patrons. The party engaged in combat, successfully defeating the mephits and subduing the drake without killing it. Afterward, they learned from **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Spark Scale\|Spark Scale]]** that **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Malachite\|Malachite]]** had passed through **[[Geography/City/Cri Chnuas\|Cri Chnuas]]** months prior and was rumored to be heading toward the **Hidden Citadel**.

The session concluded with **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Spark Scale\|Spark Scale]]** offering a trade for relics recovered from the **Hidden Citadel** and providing a map to its location. The party planned to pursue **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Malachite\|Malachite]]** while preparing for their next steps in the ongoing conflict against the [[The Tribes/Goblins\|goblins]] and the mysteries surrounding the [[The Tribes/Eladrin\|eladrin]].

## Encounters
### Market Disruption and Theft
- The party entered the **[[Geography/Locale/Scaled Market\|Scaled Market]]** and encountered [[The Tribes/Goblins\|goblin]] and [[The Tribes/Tabaxi\|tabaxi]] merchants.
- **[[The Campaign/PCs/Exploder\|Exploder]]** created a foul smell near the [[The Tribes/Goblins\|goblin]]'s stall to distract him.

- A wild magic surge caused by **Ruddy Cheeks McGee** summoned three dust mephits.
- The party engaged in combat, with **[[The Campaign/PCs/Themaer Eagerflower\|Themaer Eagerflower]]** and **[[The Campaign/PCs/Koldea\|Koldea]]** attacking the mephits.
- **[[The Campaign/PCs/Exploder\|Exploder]]** cast Magic Missile, severely injuring one mephit and killing another.
- The party subdued the **Ruddy Cheeks McGee** without killing it, learning about **[[NPCs/Contemporary/Malachite\|Malachite]]**.
