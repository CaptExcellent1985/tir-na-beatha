#### Description
> *A slender, bone-white stiletto that feels impossibly light. It creates no sound when cutting through air or cloth.*
> - **Stats:** +1 Dagger.
>  **Property (The Snip):** This weapon deals double damage to objects and structures (ideal for cutting ropes, nets, or destroying cover).  
>  **Property (Break Concentration):** When you damage a creature that is concentrating on a spell, the target has Disadvantage on the saving throw to maintain concentration.
