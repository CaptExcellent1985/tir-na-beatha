#### Description
>*A cloak made of tattered, grey silk that shimmers with iridescent dust when light hits it.*
>- **Property (Flutter):** While wearing this cloak, opportunity attacks made against you have **Disadvantage**. 
>- **Property (Soft Fall):** You can use your reaction when you fall to slow your descent (as per the _Feather Fall_ spell) by spreading the cloak like wings
