#### Description
> *A small glass vial filled with golden, sticky pollen-like dust.* 
> **Consumable - 3 Uses**
**Effect:**  As an **action**, you can blow a pinch of dust into the face of a creature within 5 feet. The creature must succeed on a **DC 13 Wisdom saving throw** or be charmed by you for 1 minute (or until you harm it). While charmed, the creature speaks in a euphoric, honest babble, giving you Advantage on Charisma checks against it.
