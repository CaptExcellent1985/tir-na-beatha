#### Summary
> A heavy wooden door separates this establishment from the bustling [[Geography/Locale/Scaled Market\|Scaled Market]]. The Ember-Belly Inn is almost always busy, filled near-to-bursting with [[02 - Tuatha\|Tuatha]] who have come to the city to do business. Offering moderately comfortable beds and overlooking and open to the [[Geography/Landmark/Cleverclaw Creek\|Cleverclaw Creek]], visitors are served up the finest of [[The Tribes/Kobolds\|Kobold]] delicacies by the current proprietor, [[NPCs/Contemporary/Spark Scale\|Spark Scale]].
