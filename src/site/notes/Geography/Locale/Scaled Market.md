#### Summary
> Located in the central cavern of [[Geography/City/Cri Chnuas\|Cri Chnuas]], the Scaled Market is often the first place that travelers to the city encounter. At the central focal point stands a statue dedicated to the God [[Religion/Pantheon/Cernunnos\|Cernunnos]], with various market stalls and tents thrown up around it. Traders from across the [[01 -Tir Na Beatha\|Tir]] gather here to hawk their wares, and [[The Tribes/Kobolds\|Kobolds]] from the lower city are everywhere. 


#### Notable Features
###### Statue of [[Religion/Pantheon/Cernunnos\|Cernunnos]]
> This stone statue towers over the marketplace, depicting a hooded and robed figure. Laid at its feet is a clutch of eggs fashioned from copper, and it is flanked by statues of [[The Tribes/Kobolds\|Kobolds]] in various states of reverence.
