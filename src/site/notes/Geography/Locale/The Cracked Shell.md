> [[The Tribes/Kobolds\|Kobolds]] who have chosen to specialize in the art of alchemy and graduate from primary studies at the [[Geography/Locale/Clutch of the Arcane\|Clutch of the Arcane]] end up apprenticing with this alchemical shop. They will work tirelessly on their experiments in the laboratory in the rear cavern while the shop-front promises to sell "the best of the worst" to those brave enough to test these concoctions out.
