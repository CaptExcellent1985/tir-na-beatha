#### Summary
> A rare flower that grows on the banks of the [[Geography/Region/Granna Abhaine\|Granna Abhaine]] and [[Geography/Region/Tiriosann River\|Tiriosann River]], but only in the area immediately surrounding [[Geography/City/Aite-Briethe\|Aite-Briethe]]. It blooms only in the evenings when the full moon is present, and is the primary food source of the [[Cultural Matters/Physical/Lumina Moth\|Lumina Moth]]. It is a highly valuable role in the production of [[Cultural Matters/Physical/Lumina Silk\|Lumina Silk]] and is strictly regulated, with only the [[Factions/Weave-mothers\|Weave-mothers]] being allowed to grow and cultivate the flower.
